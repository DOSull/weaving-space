Mapping multivariate data

A hard problem

Many methods have been tried

Enter weavingspace

In brief

An approach based on tiling

Elements in a tiling ‘pick up’ data from area units by overlay so we can combine multiple choropleths into a single map

Giving two kinds of map of complex multi­variate data (tiled and woven)

 

Maps on the next slide are of the IMD. See: Exeter DJ, Zhao J, Crengle S, Lee A, Browne M, 2017, The New Zealand Indices of Multiple Deprivation (IMD): A new suite of indicators for social and health research in Aotearoa, New Zealand PLOS ONE 12(8) e0181260
